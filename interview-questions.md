# Customer Discovery Interview Questions

## Opening (2 min)
- Thanks for taking the time!
- I'm building software for tax incentive compliance
- Want to understand your current workflow before I build the wrong thing
- This is just about learning, not selling anything

## Current Process (8 min)
1. Walk me through your last project that involved tax incentives
   - Which jurisdictions were you evaluating?
   - How did you track requirements?
   
2. What tools/resources do you use now?
   - Spreadsheets? Software? Consultants?
   
3. What takes the most time in the compliance process?
   
4. What's the most frustrating/painful part?

5. How often do incentive rules change and how do you stay updated?

## Solution Validation (3 min)
6. If there was software that [solved their biggest pain point], would you use it?

7. What would make it worth paying for vs. doing it yourself?

8. What would a realistic budget be for a tool like this?

## Wrap Up (2 min)
9. Is there anyone else I should talk to who deals with this?

10. Can I follow up with you as I build this out?
